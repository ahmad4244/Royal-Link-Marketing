Frequently Asked Questions

We’re here to help with all your real estate marketing and investment inquiries. Here are some common questions we get from clients.

1. What services does Royal Link Marketing provide?

We specialize in real estate marketing, investment consultancy, and project promotion. We help clients invest in top housing and commercial projects with verified documentation.

2. Do you help clients buy or sell individual properties?

No, we do not list or manage individual properties. We focus on guiding clients in investing in trusted and approved real estate projects or societies.

3. What kind of projects do you market?

We market both residential and commercial projects, including plots, apartments, farmhouses, and commercial outlets. All projects are vetted for approvals and documentation.

4. Can you guide me on safe real estate investment?

Yes, our experts provide personalized investment consultancy based on your goals and budget. We recommend legally approved and high-potential projects only.

5. Do you charge for consultancy?

Our initial consultation is free. Any professional charges are discussed transparently before proceeding with transactions or services.

6. How can I contact Royal Link Marketing?

You can reach us via phone, WhatsApp, or our website contact form. We’re happy to schedule meetings and share project details with interested clients.
